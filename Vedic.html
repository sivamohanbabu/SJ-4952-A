Brahma Purana

Describes the creation of the cosmos, sacred geography, and pilgrimage sites. It emphasizes rituals and the spiritual significance of temples, guiding devotees in dharma and spiritual practice.

Padma Purana

Narrates the birth of Brahma and the universe's inception, with 55,000 shlokas. It provides guidance on festivals, rituals, and the significance of months for spiritual practice.

Vishnu Purana

Focuses on Vishnu’s avatars and cosmology, detailing creation, maintenance, and destruction. It offers paths to moksha (liberation) through devotion and Vedic teachings.

Shiva Purana

Glorifies Shiva, detailing his manifestations, rituals, and the Shiva Linga. It emphasizes bhakti, asceticism, and spiritual knowledge for liberation.

Bhagavata Purana

Revered for its focus on Krishna’s life and bhakti. It narrates Vishnu’s avatars and provides spiritual insights for devotion and ethical living.

Narada Purana

Contains philosophical discussions, deity stories, and bhakti guidance. It offers insights into devotion and spiritual practices for daily life.

Markandeya Purana

Includes the Devi Mahatmyam, focusing on the Divine Mother. It guides worship of goddesses and explores myths, particularly for Navaratri rituals.

Agni Purana

An encyclopedic text covering rituals, astrology, grammar, and philosophy. It provides practical guidance for spiritual and worldly pursuits.

Bhavishya Purana

Focuses on pilgrimage guides and future predictions. It serves as a handbook for spiritual travel and understanding cosmic cycles.

Brahmavaivarta Purana

Centers on Krishna and Radha, emphasizing their divine love. It offers spiritual teachings on devotion and the supremacy of Krishna.

Linga Purana

Explores Shiva’s linga symbolism and creation myths. It provides rituals and stories for devotees seeking spiritual growth.

Varaha Purana

A guide for Vishnu worship, focusing on the Varaha avatar. It includes pilgrimage guides for Mathura and Nepal and stories of Shiva and Durga.

Skanda Purana

The largest Purana, focusing on Skanda (Kartikeya) and Vishnu’s avatars. It serves as a pilgrimage guide and narrates cosmic balance.

Vamana Purana

Details Vishnu’s Vamana avatar and human development. It simplifies Vedic and Upanishadic teachings for spiritual growth.

Kurma Purana

Focuses on Vishnu’s Kurma avatar and the Samudra Manthan. It teaches patience, balance, and devotion through mythology and rituals.

Matsya Purana

Narrates Vishnu’s Matsya avatar and the great deluge. It emphasizes knowledge preservation and divine protection.

Garuda Purana

Explains ancestral rites, moksha, and geography. It guides devotees in rituals for liberation and understanding cosmic order.

Brahmanda Purana

Details cosmic creation, time, and geography. It includes Lalita Sahasranamam and serves as a guide for goddess worship and cosmology.
