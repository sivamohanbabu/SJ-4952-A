About Sattjiva

Sattjiva is a wellness initiative based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, focusing on integrating traditional wellness practices with modern technological insights. It aims to provide a holistic approach to life through yoga, meditation, and mantra sadhanas.

Founder

The initiative is led by Siva Veludurthi, a multifaceted professional combining expertise in technology with a passion for yoga and wellness. His background includes roles as a Master Trainer certified in AI, ML, Data Science, UX Design, and MERN stack development. Siva's holistic approach aims to offer comprehensive well-being solutions to the community.

Key Offerings

  • Om Sadhana Sessions: These sessions emphasize the significance of the Om chant, aiming to align one's energy and foster deep meditation.
  • Mantra Sadhana: Explore the transformative power of Vedic mantras to achieve inner peace and spiritual growth.
  • Guru Teachings: Learning ancient wisdom through the guidance of enlightened masters.
  • Yoga and Wellness: A balanced approach to physical and mental health through guided yoga sessions.
  • Community Engagement: Sattjiva actively participates in local spiritual events, reflecting a commitment to community involvement and spiritual enrichment.
